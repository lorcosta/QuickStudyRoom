Quick StudyRoom

A fast application to book seats on study rooms around the world.

It’s always a problem for you to find a quiet place to study? Or maybe you have a free place that you want to use to make some earnings? Quick Study Room is made just for you both! You can book a study room or you can earn by renting seats in a free place in your possession.

Are you looking for a study room?

Always in a rush to find a seat in the study rooms? With Quick Study Room you can easily book a seat in the closest study room to your house and never miss a study session! You can also check all the services proposed by the study room so you can choose the best solution for you.

Do you own a study room?

Do you have a free place that you don’t use? Or do you simply want to make some money by managing a study room for students? You just need to put some tables and some chairs and Quick Study Room will do the rest.
